Who are we?

We’re a platform built by freelancers, for freelancers — designed to help providers like you manage appointments, stay connected with clients, and grow your business, all in one place. With intuitive scheduling and real-time SMS notifications, staying organized has never been easier. Our built-in analytics offer clear insights into cancellation trends, repeat no-shows, and client behavior, so you can make smarter business decisions. Whether you’re just starting out or scaling up, our powerful yet easy-to-use tools are crafted to support your success — and we’re just getting started.

What is the cost?

We believe in simple, transparent pricing. Our service starts at just $10/month, with an additional $0.03 USD per SMS notification sent to your clients from the previous month. Your first month is a flat $10 — no extra fees. If you decide to cancel, your final invoice will only include the cost of any notifications sent. Taxes and payment processing fees may apply.

What kind of providers use this?

We serve a wide range of service-based professionals — from fitness coaches and massage therapists to consultants, stylists, and tutors. If you rely on client appointments to run your business, this platform is built for you.

Do I need to install anything?

Not at all. Our platform is completely web-based, so there’s nothing to download or install. You can access your account anytime, from anywhere — no apps or updates required.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your trust is our top priority. We follow industry-leading security practices, including encryption of sensitive data, regular audits, and secure data storage standards. Whether it’s your information or your client’s, we treat it with the highest level of care and protection.

Is my credit card data secure?

Yes — we never handle or store your credit card information ourselves. All payments are processed securely through our trusted partner Stripe, a global leader in secure online payments. Your financial data stays encrypted and protected at every step.

We’ve got your back. You can reach out to us anytime at example@example.com and we’ll get back to you as quickly as possible. We’re here to support you every step of the way.
